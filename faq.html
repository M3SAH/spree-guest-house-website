Guest Support

Booking

How do I book directly?

Use the booking page, WhatsApp button, phone number, or email to request availability.

Are online rates final?

Displayed rates are indicative. The team confirms final rates, availability, and add-ons directly.

Rooms

What room types are available?

Classic Guest Rooms, Executive Rooms, and Self-Catering Suites are presented on the rooms page.

Do rooms include WiFi?

WiFi is listed as an available amenity. Confirm any specific needs while booking.

Events

Can Spree host weddings?

Yes, intimate weddings and garden events can be discussed with the team.

Do you host corporate meetings?

Yes, meetings, briefings, workshops, and conferences can be arranged by inquiry.

Dining

Is breakfast available?

Breakfast service is part of the dining experience and should be confirmed with your booking.

Can dining support events?

Yes, catering and meal support can be discussed for private functions.

Check-in is listed from 13:00 and check-out by 10:00. Confirm final details when booking.

Can I ask for transport support?

Yes, request transport guidance or shuttle support through the contact form or WhatsApp.

Payments

How are payments handled?

Payment terms are confirmed directly by Spree Guest House during booking.

Can I amend my booking?

Contact the team as early as possible by WhatsApp, phone, or email.
